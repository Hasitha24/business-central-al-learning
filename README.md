# Business Central AL Learning Journey (60 Days Challenge)

## About Me

I am a Business Central professional currently working as a Functional Consultant with hands-on experience in ERP implementations and system configuration.

My background includes:

- 1 year experience as a Project Manager in Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central projects
- 1 year experience as a Business Central Functional Consultant (current role)
- 3 months of training and hands-on practice in AL development

Through my experience in ERP implementations, I developed a strong interest in the technical side of Business Central, especially extension development using AL.

This repository documents my journey to strengthen my technical skills and move toward a **Business Central Technical Consultant / Developer role**.

---

## Goal of This Repository

The goal of this repository is to:

- Practice AL development consistently
- Build Business Central extensions
- Understand the technical architecture of Business Central
- Improve problem-solving skills through daily exercises
- Maintain a public record of my learning progress

This repository is part of a **60-day AL development practice challenge**.

---

During this journey I will be practicing:
